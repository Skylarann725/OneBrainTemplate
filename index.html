Our Services

One Brain Learning Center is a tutoring center that invigorates the entire brain by creatively enhancing the techniques needed to succeed. We have certified and qualified individuals that are equipped with the knowledge and strategies to allow students to feel comfortable with the content needed. One Brain Learning Center is currently a mobile tutoring company. The individuals here will come to you for your convenience and comfort. Typically, One Brain tutors will meet clients at a public library or a place that has internet capability. One Brain Learning Center specializes in grade school subjects ranging from elementary school to high school. College courses and career preparation tools and strategies are available upon request. Contact us today to schedule your hour session.

Meet the Tutors

Stephnie Sahadeo, owner of One Brain Learning Center, is a certified teacher in the State of Florida. She is certified in Elementary Education K-6th grade and also Exceptional Student Education K- 12th grade, with both an ESOL endorsement and a Reading endorsement. Stephnie has been teaching for 7 years and counting in Orange County, Florida. She has taught 1st grade, 4th grade, and 5th grade. She is also associated with Orlando Technical and Westside Technical school, teaching adult education GED and ESOL classes. She uses unique learning and teaching styles to help bridge any learning gaps, such as yoga and meditation. Stephnie is extremely passionate about educating all minds to succeed and grow in any way possible.

Amanda Rebeiro is a certified Exceptional Education Teacher. Amanda has worked with students with a variety of learning disabilities from kindergarten to 5th grade. She has been working in public schools in Florida for the past 5 years. Before she became a teacher, she interned in Australia, where she learned specific strategies to help all learners. Amanda is very patient with students and she is very dedicated to their growth towards personal educational goals.
